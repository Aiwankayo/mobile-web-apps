Starters

Bakso

Bakso is a popular Indonesian dish made of meatballs—typically beef, but sometimes chicken, fish, or a mix—served in a flavorful, clear broth. It’s a street food staple and comfort food favorite all over Indonesia.

Gado Gado

Gado-gado is basically Indonesia’s version of a hearty, veggie-loaded salad—but with a rich, peanut sauce dressing that takes it to a whole new level.
